About SARC IIT Bombay

Welcome to the official website of the Student Alumni Relations Cell (SARC) at IIT Bombay. We are a dedicated team of students working towards fostering strong and meaningful connections between the students and alumni of our prestigious institute.

Our Mission

At SARC, our mission is to establish a lifelong bond between the students and alumni community. We strive to create a platform that enables knowledge sharing, mentorship, and professional networking opportunities for our students. By bridging the gap between the present and past generations of IIT Bombay, we aim to facilitate the holistic development and success of our students.

What We Do

SARC organizes a wide range of events and initiatives throughout the year to strengthen the relationship between the students and alumni. These include guest lectures, alumni interaction sessions, workshops, alumni reunions, career guidance programs, and much more.

Benefits for Students

By actively participating in SARC activities, students can benefit from the vast experience and knowledge of our alumni. They gain insights into various industries, receive career guidance, and get access to internship and job opportunities. SARC also provides a platform for students to build connections, seek mentorship, and learn from the achievements of our esteemed alumni.

Get Involved

We encourage all students and alumni to actively engage with SARC and contribute to the growth and development of the IIT Bombay community. Whether you are a current student or an alumnus, there are various ways to get involved, such as becoming a member, volunteering for events, sharing your success stories, or providing mentorship to the students.

Explore our website to learn more about SARC, our upcoming events, and how you can get involved. We look forward to connecting with you and creating a thriving network of IIT Bombay students and alumni!
