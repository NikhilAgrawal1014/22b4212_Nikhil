Our Services

At SARC IIT Bombay, we offer a range of services aimed at bridging the gap between students and alumni, fostering professional development, and providing networking opportunities. Take a look at our key services below:

Alumni Interaction Sessions

We organize regular alumni interaction sessions where students get the opportunity to engage with successful alumni from various fields. These sessions provide valuable insights, career guidance, and a chance to learn from the experiences of our esteemed alumni.

Mentorship Programs

Our mentorship programs connect students with experienced alumni mentors who provide guidance and support in their academic and professional journey. Through one-on-one interactions, mentorship helps students gain valuable insights, set goals, and make informed career choices.

Career Guidance

We provide comprehensive career guidance programs that include workshops, seminars, and panel discussions conducted by industry experts and alumni. These sessions cover various career paths, job search strategies, interview preparation, and skill development to equip students for a successful career ahead.

Internship and Job Opportunities

Through our strong alumni network and collaborations with companies, we strive to bring valuable internship and job opportunities to our students. We organize recruitment drives, job fairs, and facilitate internships to connect students with top companies and organizations.

Alumni Reunions

We organize alumni reunions to bring together past graduates and provide a platform for networking, reminiscing, and sharing experiences. These reunions foster a sense of belonging, strengthen alumni connections, and provide opportunities for collaborations and knowledge sharing.

Industry Connect

We facilitate interactions between students and professionals from various industries through industry connect programs. These programs include guest lectures, industry visits, and workshops to provide students with exposure to real-world scenarios, emerging trends, and industry insights.

Explore our services to discover how SARC can support your personal and professional growth. We are dedicated to creating valuable experiences and opportunities for the IIT Bombay community!
